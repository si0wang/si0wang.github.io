About me

I am a second year Ph.D. student in University of Maryland, College Park, where I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Furong Huang. My research interest is foundation models and reinforcement learning, including world models for robotics, sample-efficient rl methods, visual rl, hallucination in large vision-language models, etc. My long-term goal is to make agents more efficient and reliable. Before that, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Network Engineering from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). I am also fortunate to work closely with Dr. Huazhe Xu from Tsinghua University and Dr. Huaxiu Yao from UNC.

I am looking for internship opportunities. Here are my CV. Please feel free to contact me if you think I could be a good fit.
